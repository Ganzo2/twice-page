Who is TWICE?

One in a Million

TWICE is a nine-member South Korean girl group formed by JYP Entertainment through the reality survival show SIXTEEN. The group — Jihyo, Nayeon, Jeongyeon, Momo, Sana, Mina, Dahyun, Chaeyoung, and Tzuyu — debuted on October 20, 2015 with the mini album The Story Begins. Known for anthemic pop hits, candy-colored visuals, and tight-knit choreography, TWICE has grown into one of the most influential K-pop acts of their generation, selling out arenas and stadiums across Asia, North America, and Europe. Their fandom is affectionately named ONCE — because loving TWICE once means they'll love you back twice as much.
